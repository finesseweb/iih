          <p>Super Hoze Hydraulic hoses are long lasting and durable, with high performance transfer capabilities. The range covers SAE100R1, R2, R3, R6, R5R, R5C, Jack Hose etc. and can be segregated on the basis of Pressure Ratings & Fluid/Material to be conveyed.</p>
          <p>Additionally, oil-resistant wire braided hoses can provide flexible connections between steel pipes in hydraulic systems. They can find their applications in the fields of mine hydraulic bracket, automatic hydraulic systems of oil field exploiting machine, mining projects and other industrial sectors - especially used as jackhammer component. These hoses can withstand temperatures from -40°C to +100°C, the maximum temperature being +135°C.</p>
